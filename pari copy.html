The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence & Data Science) nurtures future leaders to explore machine learning, data analytics, and software engineering.
PRAYAG 24
Prayag is a premier event organized by the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Adi Shankara Institute of Engineering and Technology as part of their outreach program. The event's primary goal is to introduce high school students to the fascinating world of Artificial Intelligence. Through interactive workshops, live demonstrations, and hands-on activities, this festival aims to make AI accessible and engaging for young learners, sparking their interest and curiosity in this exciting field.
